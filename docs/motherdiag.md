# MotherDiag Editions

MotherDiag is handled as a separate product family from CockpitDiag Enterprise.

## Edition Overview

| Edition | Status | Activation | Positioning |
|---|---|---|---|
| MotherDiag Community Edition | Free proprietary edition | No `license.json` required | Basic Windows diagnostic, information and simple reports |
| MotherDiag Technician Edition | Professional proprietary edition | `license.json` may be required for advanced/protected functions | Technician and workshop-oriented workflows |

## MotherDiag Community Edition

MotherDiag Community Edition is the free proprietary edition of MotherDiag. It is not open source and it is not a Technician licence.

Community is intended for simpler diagnostic, information and basic report workflows. It does not require activation and should not be documented as requiring `license.json`.

Dedicated page:

- [MotherDiag Community Edition](motherdiag-community.md)
- [MotherDiag Community installation](installation-motherdiag-community.md)

## MotherDiag Technician Edition

MotherDiag Technician Edition is the professional technician edition. It is distributed separately under a proprietary professional licence. Advanced or protected functions may require a signed `license.json`.

Technician packaging and release information will be published only after final audit, obfuscation test and controlled packaging.

## Important Separation

MotherDiag Community, MotherDiag Technician and CockpitDiag Enterprise must not be mixed in documentation, packaging or licence wording.

Community is free/proprietary without activation. Technician is professional/proprietary with licence-controlled features. CockpitDiag Enterprise is a separate advanced Enterprise suite.
